Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What This Site Does

WhatChannelAreTheYankeesOn.com is a free informational site that displays the New York Yankees game schedule, broadcast information, standings, and related baseball data. We do not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information to use this site.

Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information. However, we use your browser's local storage (not cookies) to save anonymous usage data entirely on your own device. This includes a randomly generated visitor ID, page visit counts, click interactions, session duration, device type, browser type, and referral source. This data never leaves your browser and is not transmitted to any server. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for this site.

Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site or other sites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your browsing history. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. For more information about how Google uses data, visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

Third-Party APIs

This site fetches publicly available data from the MLB Stats API and the Open-Meteo weather API to display game schedules, scores, and stadium weather. These requests are made from your browser directly to those services. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

Analytics

This site uses a lightweight, client-side analytics system that stores all data locally in your browser using localStorage. It tracks anonymous usage patterns such as which sections you view, how long you spend on each, and which buttons you click. No data is sent to any external analytics service. We do not use Google Analytics. Your theme preference (light or dark mode) is also saved locally in your browser.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us at the email address listed on the site.
